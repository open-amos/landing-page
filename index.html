The problem

Disconnected data is costing you time, deals, and confidence

From Excel chaos to system silos to vendor lock-in. Too many private market teams rely on fragile workflows that slow reporting, obscure insights, and limit decision-making.

Siloed systems

Your CRM, fund admin portal, ESG tracker, and servicing platform all operate in isolation, creating gaps, duplicates, and inconsistencies between teams that should be looking at the same numbers.

Excel chaos

Data lives in spreadsheets and exports with no single source of truth. Analysts spend 30 to 40% of their time reconciling versions instead of doing the work they were hired for.

Slow, manual reporting

Quarterly reports, ESG disclosures, and LP updates take days or weeks, driven by spreadsheets, screenshots, and repeated copy-paste between systems.

Vendor lock-in

Legacy platforms charge steep fees, restrict access, and make it costly just to extract your own information when you want to change tools or strategies.

Hidden audit risk

Manual processes lead to errors, outdated data, and untraceable changes, turning every audit and regulatory review into a stressful, last-minute scramble.

Missed opportunities

Without clean, real-time data, you can't move quickly on a deal, answer an LP question on a live call, or trust the numbers behind a strategic decision.
